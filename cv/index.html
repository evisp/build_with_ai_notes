About

My work spans autonomous robotics, machine learning, and intelligent systems; as a researcher, an educator, and a practitioner who values rigour and real-world applicability in equal measure. Across a decade of research, teaching, and building, I have worked to bridge formal AI methods and real autonomous behaviour, driven by a long-term interest in systems that require ever less human guidance.
